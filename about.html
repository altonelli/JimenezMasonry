Introduction

Jimenez Masonry provides more than just masonry solutions. We are proud to be Jimenez Masonry and more. Founded 10 years ago by Dagoberto Jimenez the company has continued to grow and in January 2017 received its 8a certi cation. The company is based near Salinas, CA with management team located in both northern and southern CA to serve the entire state of California. The company management team has done a great number of projects forDOD (Army, Navy, Air Force), GSA (USGS, VA, US Marshalls, US Fed. Courts), NOAA, and FAA. The company is dedicated to providing strong management skills, with on time and on budget delivery of all its projects. The team at Jimenez Masonry has large array disciplines, in general building construction, engineering, design, Federal Government construction, high-end private sector work, as well as, commercial masonry. With the 8a direct award ability, we can provide exact requirements for your agency, as well as, your customer. The cornerstone of our business which begins with always remembering that our business it to make your job easier in a professional manner.
